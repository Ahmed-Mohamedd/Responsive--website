Our Story

Since our inception in 2012 , We have set out to disrupt the HR & talent/staffing managment industry

Purposefully designed by our founders to connect great companies and great talent,we have become a white glove boutique firm known for our agility and high operational excellence standards.

We offer unprecedented customer service by developing customized, worry-free, cost-effective and time-saving HR & Talent/Staffing Management Models for our valued clients – building thoughtful solutions based on our clients’ unique needs.

Our reputation of disrupting the norm, serving as an agile and lean recruiting provider and employer of record has contributed to our notable growth of 4,900% over three years.

With a diverse workforce of over 500+ and annual revenue right under $50M in 2018, As a Women’s Business Enterprise (WBE, WBENC, WSOB) certified, a minority-owned organization that is committed to our clients and truly talking the talk and walking the walk of diversity and inclusion.

Dedicated to bringing diversity of thought and talent to the workforce to enhance how business is done, By making connections, solving problems and serving as a partner clients can trust.

TalEx Cares: Our Social Responsibility

In the words of Nelson Mandela, "there can be no greater gift than that of giving one's time and energy to help others."

All we do at TalEx is built upon the foundation of our Values. Integrity, empathy, trust, diversity & inclusion... just to name a few. In keeping with these values, we are committed to providing an extraordinary standard of care and service not only to our people, clients, candidates and strategic partners but to our community. As a part of this commitment, we offer our time and subject matter expertise as well as 5% of net profit annually to various philanthropic organizations. To make a true positive social impact through our #TalExGlobalCitizen Campaign we aim to inspire others to consider ways they too can make a positive impact on our global community.
